The opportunity

The category leader is open.

Nigeria’s agritech sector took in over $150M between 2023 and 2025. Real-estate apps like PropertyPro and Estate Intel have normalised mobile-first ownership of physical assets. Yet no one has put the three things that matter for a Nigerian investor into a single product: titled land, installment ownership, and live farm visibility.

Why investors hesitate today

  • Title fraud is the headline risk — investors won’t move without C of O verification.
  • Crop-sponsorship platforms (FarmCrowdy, ThriveAgric) tie capital to a 6-month cycle, not lasting equity.
  • Listing marketplaces (PropertyPro) end at the contact form — no escrow, no installments, no ROI proof.
  • Diaspora buyers want a way to monitor what they own without flying in.

How Cropperty wins

  • Titled ownership — every plot ties to a verified C of O or Governor’s Consent.
  • Outright or installment — 6, 12 or 24 monthly debits, fully automated.
  • Live farm telemetry — drone snapshots, satellite, weather and yield reports.
  • Agent referral graph — every offline broker becomes a tracked, paid growth lever.
